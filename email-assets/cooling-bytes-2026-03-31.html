M&A
Vertiv Bought a Heat Exchanger Company. The Real Bottleneck Was Never at the Chip.
Vertiv acquired ThermoKey S.p.A., a 230-person Italian manufacturer of dry coolers and microchannel heat exchangers founded in 1991. EUR 60M in 2023 revenue, up 20% YoY. CEO Giordano Albertazzi: "Heat rejection is becoming increasingly critical." Liquid cooling does not eliminate heat. It concentrates it. The outdoor rejection equipment at most sites was never designed for what AI racks are dumping on it. Vertiv posted $10.23B in 2025 revenue with a $15B backlog.

TECHNOLOGY
The Power Electronics Inside Your Chiller Are Holding Back Efficiency.
SemiQ launched the QSiC Dual3: 1200V SiC MOSFET modules for 250kW liquid chiller motor drives. 240 W/in³ power density. Replacing IGBTs with SiC yields 2.4% efficiency gain, 50% reduction in switching losses. That is 6kW saved per chiller. Every die screened above 1,450V at wafer level. President Timothy Han is positioning it as an IGBT drop-in. The component inside the cooling system that nobody talks about.

INFRASTRUCTURE
Cooling Is the #1 Risk in Commissioning a 100MW AI Data Center
Applied Digital COO Laura Laltrello and VP of Engineering Stephen Lattimer: mechanical cooling is the greatest operational vulnerability at hyperscale. Commissioning starts 30-45 days after groundbreaking. Their zero-water chiller in North Dakota runs three modes. Every transition is a potential failure point. Cooling vendors who show up with a spec sheet are already behind.

TECHNOLOGY
Liquid Cooling Fixed the GPU Problem. Now Storage Is Overheating.
Every NVMe SSD ever built assumed airflow. In NVIDIA's fanless GB200 NVL72 racks, drives sit in still air with no thermal path. PCIe Gen 6 could push 40-60W per drive. Solidigm shipped the first cold-plate cooled enterprise SSD. Wiwynn built a fanless 96-drive chassis. The constraint on AI infrastructure is no longer compute. It is system-level thermal design.
