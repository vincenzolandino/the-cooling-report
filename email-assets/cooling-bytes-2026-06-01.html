DOE just wrote geothermal and Cold UTES into the AI data center plan.

Cold Underground Thermal Energy Storage is the quiet line item. It banks chilled water in the subsurface during low-demand hours and pulls it back at peak. That shrinks the chiller plant and cuts the power required for cooling, the same lever the Penn State controller captures in software.
